About This Video

This educational video explores how energy flows through living systems, from the sun to plants and animals. Students will learn about photosynthesis, food chains, and energy pyramids in an engaging, easy-to-understand format.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understand how energy enters living systems
  • Identify the role of producers, consumers, and decomposers
  • Explain energy transfer in food chains
  • Recognize the importance of the sun as the primary energy source
