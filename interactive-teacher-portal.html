Bundle 1: Organism Structures & Behavior

Lesson 1: Introduction to Organism Structures
Learn about basic structures of plants and animals and how they help organisms survive.
Lesson 2: Plant Structures and Functions
Explore plant parts, photosynthesis, and root systems with interactive activities.
Lesson 3: Animal Structures and Adaptations
Compare animal structures across species and understand adaptation mechanisms.
Lesson 4: Plant Structure Investigation Lab
Hands-on plant dissection with microscope examination and scientific drawing.
Lesson 5: Food Chains and Energy Flow
Understand food chain relationships and energy flow through ecosystems.
Lesson 6: Ecosystem Interactions
Explore predator-prey relationships and ecosystem balance.
Lesson 7: Human Impact on Ecosystems
Conservation, pollution, and sustainability practices.
Lesson 8: Ecosystem Survey Lab
Field survey, species identification, data collection, and analysis.
Lesson 9: Adaptation and Evolution
Natural selection and adaptation case studies.
Lesson 10: Biodiversity and Classification
Taxonomy and ecosystem diversity.
Lesson 11: Life Cycles and Reproduction
Metamorphosis and reproduction strategies.
Lesson 12: Life Cycle Observation Lab
Longitudinal observation and timeline creation.

Bundle 2: Earth's Surface & Resources

Lesson 13: Introduction to Earth's Surface
Explore mountains, valleys, plains, and other landforms that shape our planet.
Lesson 14: Weathering and Erosion
Understand how wind, water, and ice change Earth's surface over time.
Lesson 15: Rocks and Minerals
Identify different types of rocks and minerals and their formation processes.
Lesson 16: Rock and Mineral Investigation Lab
Hands-on identification using tools, tests, and classification charts.
Lesson 17: Soil Formation and Types
Learn about soil layers, formation, and how different soils support plant life.
Lesson 18: Water Cycle and Weather
Explore evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and weather patterns.
Lesson 19: Natural Disasters
Study earthquakes, volcanoes, floods, and how they change the landscape.
Lesson 20: Earth Changes Simulation Lab
Model volcanic eruptions, earthquakes, and erosion using materials.
Lesson 21: Fossil Formation and Evidence
Discover how fossils form and what they tell us about Earth's history.
Lesson 22: Natural Resources and Conservation
Explore renewable vs. non-renewable resources and conservation practices.
Lesson 23: Human Impact on Earth
Analyze pollution, deforestation, and environmental protection strategies.
Lesson 24: Environmental Monitoring Lab
Collect and analyze environmental data to assess local ecosystem health.
Lesson 25: Climate Change and Earth's Future
Study climate patterns, greenhouse effect, and future environmental challenges.
Lesson 26: Earth Science Review and Assessment
Comprehensive review of Earth science concepts and skills assessment.

Bundle 3: Energy & Forces

Lesson 27: Introduction to Energy
Explore different forms of energy and how energy transforms from one type to another.
Lesson 28: Motion and Forces Investigation Lab
Hands-on experiments with push, pull, friction, and force measurement.
Lesson 29: Simple Machines
Investigate levers, pulleys, inclined planes, and how they make work easier.
Lesson 30: Simple Machines Design Lab
Build and test simple machines to solve everyday problems.
Lesson 31: Sound and Vibrations
Explore how sound travels, pitch, volume, and musical instruments.
Lesson 32: Light and Vision Investigation Lab
Hands-on experiments with light, reflection, refraction, and color mixing.
Lesson 33: Heat and Temperature
Investigate thermal energy, conduction, convection, and radiation.
Lesson 34: Energy Transfer Investigation Lab
Design experiments to measure and observe energy transformations.
Lesson 35: Electricity and Circuits
Build circuits, understand current flow, and explore electrical safety.
Lesson 36: Magnetism and Electromagnets Investigation Lab
Hands-on experiments with magnetic fields, attraction, repulsion, and electromagnets.
Lesson 37: Renewable Energy Sources
Study solar, wind, hydroelectric power and sustainable energy solutions.
Lesson 38: Energy Solutions Challenge Lab
Design and build renewable energy systems to power model communities.

Bundle 4: Engineering & Design

Lesson 39: Introduction to Engineering Design
Learn the engineering design process and problem-solving strategies.
Lesson 40: Problem-Solving Design Lab
Hands-on engineering design process to solve real-world problems.
Lesson 41: Materials and Their Properties
Test material strength, flexibility, and choose best materials for designs.
Lesson 42: Bridge Building Challenge Lab
Design and build bridges using limited materials to support maximum weight.
Lesson 43: Prototyping and Testing
Build prototypes, test designs, and improve based on results.
Lesson 44: Communication Technology Lab
Hands-on experiments with communication devices and technology.
Lesson 45: Technology and Innovation
Explore how technology solves problems and improves daily life.
Lesson 46: Invention Convention Lab
Create an original invention to solve a real-world problem.
Lesson 47: Environmental Engineering
Design solutions for environmental challenges and sustainability.
Lesson 48: Engineering Portfolio Lab
Final hands-on lab to compile and present engineering design portfolios.
